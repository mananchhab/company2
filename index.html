GOOD TIMES - WE ARE HIRING

Who we are interested in hiring For apprentice scheme??

• Engineering graduates interested in Core jobs Graduates interested to work in Metro, high speed and Rail industry
• Engineering graduates looking to build a career in major rail driven countries like United Kingdom, Australia, Canada and European countries.
• Engineering graduates interested to get in to stable career, where experience adds on year by year and develops them into a great asset as they get old unlike software industry where you will be a liability to the organisation.
• Engineering graduates interested to switch to a stable career, they can attend training remotely and attend site visit in person for planned day.

What skills you will achieve after training?

• UK / Indian Rail Signalling principles, project working experience, project documentation guidance and equipment practical learning.
• Metro signalling principles and practical site-based learning.
• World class professional memberships like IRSE and IET will be awarded.
• European train control systems principles.
• Related software training eg. MicroStation, Plan software, simulation-based learning.
• Includes site visits to Metro and Rail facilities and control centres.
• Equipment’s will be available for display during tutorials.
