    About Us

Welcome to Sigtech Solutions, specialists in the delivery of railway signalling Designs and training programmes to join the Rail Signalling Industry. Sigtech Solutions has been set up by industry people with an excellent reputation in the Signal Engineering discipline both in the India and internationally. Our fields of expertise include Technical Training and Development, Competence Assurance and Technical Consultancy.

Offering excellent value for money
Encouraging, training merit students via stipend based apprentice scheme
Aligning training to meet each customers requirements
Ensuring that each individual trainees needs are met
Only employing fully qualified and competent trainers
Guiding trainees to secure a job in the industry
