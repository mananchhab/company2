Research And Development

The Institute of Railway Research (IRR) within the sigtech is a leading centre in the field of railway engineering and safety.

Our research has helped to improve safety and knowledge of the way in which railway signalling interact with the train including key performance aspects such as level crossing safety improvement. In collaboration with industry and academic partners, this work has led to a number of new tools and techniques which are now used to provide a warning on the unamend crossing, to increase safety and reliability levels, reduce cost and improve performance of the railway system.

A large proportion of the (Sigtech Railway Research)SRR’s research is of special relevance to the agendas of safety, sustainability and carbon reduction. For example, new materials, manufacturing technologies and systems of maintenance and condition-monitoring can increase cost-effectiveness for rail operators, and the transition to electrification can be accelerated by research that will make overhead power distribution much more efficient and reliable.
