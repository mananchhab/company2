FAQ

1Where is Sigtech main office based?

Our main office is based at Stapleford, UK and Indian office is based in Bhopal, New Delhi.

2 How to register for a course?

Step 1. You need to contact us.
Step 2. You will be called for initial assessment.
Step 3. you will be called for the interview.

3What level of academic record is needed to be selected for training?

Engineering graduates with academic percentage of at least 70%, have good chances for selection. Overall selection is based on candidates understanding of concepts and enthusiasm to learn.

4 How will you pay stippend and how often?

We credit stipend every month into candidate bank account.

5 Will I be able to receive online training?

Yes you will be able to receive training online, but in person attendance is required for site visits.

6 If I dont get selected will i be still able to receive training?

Yes, we provide training for interested candidates. Please check fee details in the payment section.

7 What are the benfit for an experience person to join Sigtech?

Experienced people will receive complete guidance about network rail process, hierarchy, documentation, license assessment and guidance. They will have the benefit to interact with people working in UK, who will guide them to achieve next level in their career .
